Roadblocks on the Path to an Electric Future

Despite the surge in adoption, challenges remain. One of the biggest hurdles is infrastructure.

While EV sales are growing rapidly, charging infrastructure often lags behind, creating "charging deserts" in many regions.

Another pressing issue is the environmental impact of EV production, particularly the mining of key battery materials like lithium and cobalt.

EV adoption is outpacing the growth of charging infrastructure.
